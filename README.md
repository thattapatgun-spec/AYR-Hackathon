# Description:
Ayr(= is a AI chatting bot that was created to lessen the affects from mental health issue in modern society. Ayr(= was developed to be as human-like as possible to be a companion that can relieve stress and stay by everyone side especially the one with mental issue.

# Features
• Detection: Detect mood and preferences from voice , text and biometric data.
- mood analysis
• Adaptation: Use the detected data to adapt interaction to suit users’ mood and psychological status.
• Real life information: ability to interact first like sending reels and videos by collecting data from users’ preferences.
- Provide advice on methods or places that suit the user's emotional state at that moment, based on the permissions granted for accessing their location.
• Privacy: Your conversation stay only with you and yourself no judgement by anyone.
